ABOUT PRIME SURPLUS RECOVERY

A Trusted Recovery Partner You Can Rely On

Prime Surplus Recovery is a professional research and document preparation firm specializing in locating and helping individuals recover surplus and unclaimed funds from property sales and court-held fund registries across the United States.

When a property is sold at a tax deed sale, foreclosure auction, or sheriff's sale for more than the amount owed, the excess proceeds legally belong to the prior owner or their heirs.

We bridge that gap. Our team conducts thorough public records research, identifies eligible claimants, and prepares all required documentation to support a successful claim — with complete confidentiality and zero upfront fees.
